# JetJot

JetJot is a lightweight word processor focused on elegant, distraction-free writing, built with C# and Avalonia.

Its interface emphasizes clarity and balance over excess.

## Philosophy

Rather than adding unnecessary and often unused features, JetJot borrows the best ideas from tools like iA Writer and Scrivener, combining them into something free and open source that gets out of your way.

The goal is a writing environment that feels thoughtful, calm, and enjoyable to use.

## Features

- **Manuscript-based organization** - Structure your writing
- **Session restoration** - Pick up exactly where you left off
- **Multiple focus modes** - Minimize distractions with customizable views
- **Word goals & tracking** - Set targets and watch your progress
- **Customizable UI** - Choose your font, size, and color theme. Hide elements you don't need.
- **Cross-platform** - Windows, macOS, Linux
